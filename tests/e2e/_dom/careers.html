Working at spriteCloud

Our software testing and automation consultancy company was founded in 2009 by three testers. They set out to create a company where testers could excel. A workplace that was enjoyable and focused. A home for those with testing in their DNA.

Talk To Our Team
Our Company Values
We Are Social

We are communicative and easy to get along with. Any excuse to get together and have a good time is a good one for us! We host several events throughout the year and encourage our colleagues to get together to connect around common interests.

We believe in clear, frequent, and transparent communication. At spriteCloud, sharing knowledge, information, and informed opinions is how we grow - together.

We Are Open
We Are Passionate

We do what we love with intensity and drive. Passion fuels our innovation, commitment, and enthusiasm for helping our clients succeed.

Quality is in the details - especially when no one is watching. Like Henry Ford said, we take pride in doing it right the first time, every time.

We Breathe Quality
We Are Respectful

We honour the needs, expectations, and contributions of others. Respect guides how we collaborate - with empathy, inclusion, and integrity.

Our Office

You can find us in Amsterdam-West, just a stone’s throw from station Lelylaan, in the award winning Westbeat complex.

Designed for flexibility, our space includes dedicated work areas for our testing and support teams, and a versatile co-working area with movable furniture to accommodate various activities—from performance testing workshops and large team meetings to social events.
