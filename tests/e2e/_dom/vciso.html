vCISO

Virtual Cyber Security Information Officer

A cyber security officer calls for a unique blend of extensive subject expertise and practical hands-on experience in the field. The cybersecurity realm faces a shortage of proficient professionals, intensifying the demand for individuals equipped with valuable skills and a wealth of experience.

Book A Call
Our spriteCloud virtual chief information security officer (vCISO) provides all the expertise needed to get started with real IT Governance maturity work from Day-1, at a fraction of the cost of a CISO.

‍Our vCISO service offers:
  • Traditional staff augmentation, enabling a presence for you in online meetings, events, operations and strategy planning.
  • Consultative engagement and management to drive creation and implementation of security and risk program artefacts, such as strategic and tactical roadmaps, architecture, and policy, and to run risk management and risk assessment processes.
  • Project management of architecting and deployment of security and risk solutions
  • Coaching or advisory services to train full-time staff on how to leverage created artefacts, develop communicating plans and train the next generation of security and risk leaders.
Overall, virtual cybersecurity officers offer expertise, cost-effectiveness, round-the-clock monitoring, proactive risk management, customised solutions, scalability, and regulatory compliance. These benefits make them valuable assets for organisations aiming to bolster their cybersecurity posture and protect their digital assets effectively.

Benefits of using a vCISO

1 — Cost-effectiveness

Hiring a full-time, on-site cybersecurity officer can be expensive. Our virtual CISO service provides a cost-effective solution by offering this services at a fraction of the cost. Additionally, they can work on a flexible basis, allowing our partners to scale your cybersecurity needs according to your budget.

2 — Expertise and Specialisation

Virtual cybersecurity officers possess specialised knowledge and expertise in various areas of cybersecurity. They stay up-to-date with the latest threats, technologies, and best practices, ensuring that our partners receive high-quality advice and guidance to mitigate risks effectively.

3 — Proactive Risk Management

Virtual cybersecurity officers focus on proactive risk management strategies. They assess an organisation's infrastructure, identify vulnerabilities, and implement appropriate security controls to prevent potential threats.

4 — Scalability and Flexibility

As your businesses and cybersecurity needs grows and evolve, our vCISO service can easily scale accordingly. We can adapt to changing requirements, deploy additional resources and accommodate fluctuations in workload.

5 — Enhanced Compliance and Regulation Adherence

Our vCISOs are well-versed in compliance frameworks and industry regulations. They help organisations adhere to relevant security standards,  and ensure that their cybersecurity practices align with legal and regulatory requirements. This expertise reduces the risk of non-compliance penalties and repetitional damage.

Common Questions About vCISOs

What does your vCISO do for me?

We will provide you with a highly experienced CISO resource with at least ten years of experience in the IT security and governance domain. They will be your expert mentor, project manager and top-level resource to get momentum behind your efforts to comply with industry certifications that interest you, such as ISO27001, ISO9001 and HIPAA.

Will our vCISO do all the work needed to get a certification?

No, not everything. Your vCISO will be like the head football coach; they will help assemble an internal team from your staff, review your current assets and work, and do the heavy lifting in making sense of certification detail and converting it to action plans. But your own staff will need to do routine work in completing written detail for certification components.

Do you bring ISO certification documentation with you?

Yes, we will bring all the documentation for certification with us. You will not be asked to pay for this separately.

How long will it take for our vCISO to get us certified?

There is no one answer to this question. It depends on how many resources you put into a team that will work with the vCISO and what your foundation of work is. If you start from scratch with policy work and have a small team, it will take you much longer than if you have been working on policies for several years, and you can assemble a decent team to push the work forward. It’s always best to talk to us directly so we can help with assessments on specific certifications.
